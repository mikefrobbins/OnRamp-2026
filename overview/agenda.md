# OnRamp agenda

Welcome to the OnRamp agenda. This four-day program delivers a structured introduction to
PowerShell and modern IT automation through instructor-led sessions, demonstrations, and guided
labs.

Each day builds on the last, moving from PowerShell fundamentals and command discovery to scripting,
remoting, source control, reusable tooling, debugging, and real-world automation. Attendees progress
together through the OnRamp track, gaining practical skills they can immediately apply in their
environments.

> [!NOTE]
> The schedule is subject to change.

## Monday 13 April

**Focus:** Getting Started, Discoverability, and Core Pipeline Concepts

Monday introduces PowerShell and its role in IT automation, then covers getting started, command
discovery, and the Help system. You'll learn how to launch PowerShell, find and understand commands,
and explore objects. The afternoon focuses on using the pipeline to build practical, readable
one-liners for everyday automation.
